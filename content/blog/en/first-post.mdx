## The New Reality of Web Performance

Core Web Vitals are no longer just an SEO ranking factor — they are a business decision. A slow website costs conversions, trust, and ultimately revenue.

Since the early 2026 update, Google now evaluates **Interaction to Next Paint (INP)** instead of First Input Delay. This means: it's not just the first click that counts, but the entire interaction chain.

### The Three Key Metrics

1. **LCP** — under 2.5 seconds
2. **INP** — under 200 milliseconds
3. **CLS** — under 0.1
